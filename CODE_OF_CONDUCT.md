# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and welcome good-faith questions.
- Give feedback about code and ideas, not people.
- Respect privacy and never share another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, trolling, publication of private information, and other conduct that creates an unsafe or hostile environment are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban participants for behavior that violates this code. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact channels.

This code applies in all project spaces and when an individual is representing the project publicly.
